# Chinese Corpus Authorization

The repository operator has stated that the Chinese corpus materials in this
folder are authorized for publication in this GitHub repository.

Publication scope:

- `texts/`: curated Chinese text extracted from authorized sources.
- `generated-texts/`: generated Chinese companion text for source-corpus use.
- `originals/`: authorized source PDFs and ebook files, stored with Git LFS.
- `manifest.json`, `generated-manifest.json`, `concept-ledger.json`: corpus
  metadata used by Hegel Salon.

Maintenance rules:

- Add new public Chinese corpus files only when redistribution rights are clear.
- Put private or uncertain materials under `data/corpus/chinese/private/` or
  `local-resources/chinese/`; both are outside the public release path.
- Keep source, translator, edition, license, and provenance notes with future
  additions whenever that information is available.
